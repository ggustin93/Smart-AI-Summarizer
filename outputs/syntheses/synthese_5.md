### What Items Impact the Webflow Page Load Speed
- **Heavy Scripts:** Demand significant processing.
- **Web Fonts:** Overabundance requiring separate downloads.
- **Uncompressed Images:** Hog bandwidth.

### Why Speed Matters For Your Webflow Site
- **User Experience:** 53% of mobile users abandon sites that take longer than three seconds to load.
- **Conversions:** A delay of even a second can lead to a 7% reduction in conversions.
- **SEO:** Google factors in load speed for search rankings.
